Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about plans and features.

1. What happens after my 3-day Free Trial ends?
After the trial expires, your account remains active but restricted. You will be prompted to choose a plan to unlock resume downloads, unlimited resumes, Smart Calendar, and AI Tutor (Knott).
2. Will I lose my data if I don't upgrade immediately?
No. All your resumes, portfolios, and role mappings remain safely stored. You can upgrade anytime to regain full access.
3. What is Target Role Access?
Target Role Access lets you select and track specific job roles you want to prepare for. Each role comes with required skills, learning paths, and readiness tracking.
4. What is AI-Powered Skill Analysis?
It analyzes your profile and compares it with your target role to show skill gaps, strengths, and improvement recommendations.
5. Is AI Tutor (Knott) included in all plans?
No. AI Tutor (Knott) is included only in the Premium and Institution / Enterprise plans. Pro users focus on core career-building tools.
6. Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
7. Do you offer discounts for students or yearly billing?
Yes. We offer discounted yearly plans and special pricing for students and institutions. Contact our sales team for details.
8. What is the Skill Matrix in the Institution / Enterprise plan?
Skill Matrix is an organization-level dashboard that tracks skills, progress, and readiness across all students or employees in one place.
9. Can institutions get custom features or integrations?
Yes. Enterprise customers can request custom workflows, integrations, reporting formats, and branding.
10. How does billing work for Institution / Enterprise plans?
Pricing is quote-based depending on number of users, features, and customization needs. A dedicated account manager will guide setup and onboarding.
